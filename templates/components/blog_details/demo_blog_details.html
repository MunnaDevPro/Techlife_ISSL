**US President Donald Trump on Saturday played down prospects of a summit with Russian leader Vladimir Putin anytime soon, even as a top Kremlin negotiator huddled for talks with US officials on ending the war in Ukraine.**

In a candid assessment of the geopolitical landscape, President Trump stated that while he respects President Putin, a formal summit would require significant, concrete progress on shared international goals. The focus remains on de-escalation in Eastern Europe and securing long-term stability.
